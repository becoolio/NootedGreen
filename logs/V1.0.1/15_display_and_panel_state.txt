## Analysis
The system shows the display is online and working at 1080p, but:
1. VRAM is only 7 MB - indicates fallback/basic framebuffer mode
2. No Metal kext is loaded - "No Kext Loaded"
3. Device is using software rendering (no acceleration)

This is a Stage 4 state: framebuffer attaches and display works, but no acceleration.
